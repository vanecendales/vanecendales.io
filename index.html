Hi, I’m Vanessa Cendales, I live in Cali and I’m 37, I’,m a graphic designer graduated from the Jorge Tadeo Lozano University in 2008, and I have been working in the area of ​​design and layout focused on web languages ​​for more than 12 years.I started as a Flash animator, because I specialized in multimedia and animation; and from there I was very closer to the code as part of the design (flash worked with Action Script 2-3) so when this program was withdrawn from the market, migrating those animations to more codeable languages ​​led me to learn another series of tools to be able to see on a screen my design as HTML, CSS, Javascript (React - VUE), PHP. and integrating it with repositories like GIT.
As a designer, she has had the chance to work in user experience teams as a product developer. In addition to creating the product, it is very important to allow it to evolve and change to improve not only a product as such, but also an entire experience and motivation. My favorite program is Figma and the adobe suite (illustrator - photoshop - XD).An important part of my work experience is Wordpress, since I have been a developer in that field for more than 8 years, alternating from template design to plugin creation, REST Api, queries and database optimization,note: all published designs are also layout by me (due to copyright issues)Thank you for your time, I hope my experience has been of interest to you.
